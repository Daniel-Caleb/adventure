Archery

Archery was best known to be used during medieval times as a means to wage war from a distance. ‘Archeress’ has been used to address female archers, but nowadays the preferred term is simply ‘archer’. With the invention of firearms, things like bows soon lost all their purposes, and Archers had to switch to firearms.
